About Hillside Strategies

Our Story

Founded in December 2024, Hillside Strategies was established to bridge the gap between policy and business in emerging markets across Africa. We recognized the need for strategic advisory services that combine local expertise with global perspective.

Our team brings together seasoned professionals with deep experience in political advocacy, government relations, and strategic consulting. We understand the complex regulatory environments across African nations and have built strong networks with key stakeholders.

At Hillside Strategies, we're committed to helping our clients navigate political landscapes, build meaningful relationships with decision-makers, and achieve their strategic objectives through ethical and effective advocacy.

Our Values

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our engagements, ensuring transparency and trust in every relationship.

Innovation
